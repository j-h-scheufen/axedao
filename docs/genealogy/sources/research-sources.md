# Capoeira Research Sources

This file documents known web sources for capoeira genealogy research. Import commands (`/import-person`, `/import-group`) should consult these sources during research.

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-15

---

## How to Use This File

When researching a person or group:
1. Start with **Tier 1** sources (highest reliability)
2. Cross-reference with **Tier 2** sources for additional detail
3. Use **Tier 3** sources for contemporary information
4. Consult **Tier 4** sources for academic/historical context

For each source, follow the specific instructions on what data can be extracted and where to find it.

---

## Tier 1: Primary Reference Sources

### Velhos Mestres (velhosmestres.com)

**URL:** https://velhosmestres.com
**Languages:** Portuguese (primary), English (/en/)
**Focus:** Historical mestres, biographical data, oral history
**Reliability:** High - curated by researchers, includes academic citations

**Data Available:**
- Birth/death dates with precision
- Teachers and students
- Family connections
- Timeline of documented incidents
- Academic source citations
- Photographs (some historical)
- Oral testimony transcriptions

**How to Access:**
- Person pages: `velhosmestres.com/en/[apelido]` or `velhosmestres.com/br/[apelido]`
- Timeline entries: `velhosmestres.com/br/[apelido]-[year]` (e.g., `/br/besouro-1918`)
- Use WebFetch with Portuguese URLs for more complete data

**Best For:** Besouro Mangangá, Cobrinha Verde, Tio Alípio, historical Bahian mestres, pre-1950 figures

---

### Wikipedia / Wikimedia

**URLs:**
- English: `en.wikipedia.org/wiki/[Name]`
- Portuguese: `pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/[Name]`
- Commons: `commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:[Name]`

**Focus:** Verified biographical information, public domain images
**Reliability:** Medium-High - requires citations, but can have errors

**Data Available:**
- Birth/death dates (often with citations)
- Basic biography
- Notable achievements
- Public domain portraits
- References to academic sources

**How to Access:**
- Search for person name + "capoeira"
- Check Portuguese Wikipedia for Brazilian figures (often more detailed)
- Use Wikimedia Commons for portraits (check license)

**Best For:** Well-known mestres (Bimba, Pastinha, Besouro), major groups, historical context

---

### CapoeiraHub (capoeirahub.net)

**URL:** https://www.capoeirahub.net
**Focus:** Lineage tree visualization, mestre profiles
**Reliability:** Medium - user-contributed, some gaps

**Data Available:**
- Structured lineage relationships ("learned from")
- Birth/death years
- Biographical text
- Group associations (limited)
- Visual lineage tree

**How to Access:**
- Mestres: `capoeirahub.net/mestres/[id]-[name]`
- Groups: Limited (~30 groups)
- Lineage tree: Navigate from mestre profiles

**Best For:** Visualizing lineage chains, cross-referencing teacher-student relationships

**Limitations:** Only ~30 groups, incomplete data, single relationship type

---

### Lalaue (lalaue.com)

**URL:** https://www.lalaue.com/learn-capoeira/
**Focus:** Educational content, mestre biographies, capoeira music
**Reliability:** Medium-High - editorial curation

**Data Available:**
- Mestre biographies: `/learn-capoeira/[name]/`
- Group overviews: `/learn-capoeira/biggest-capoeira-groups-worldwide/`
- Capoeira music lyrics with attribution
- Historical context articles

**How to Access:**
- Person: `lalaue.com/learn-capoeira/[apelido]/`
- Group: `lalaue.com/learn-capoeira/[group-name]/`

**Best For:** Besouro, Pastinha, Bimba, major historical figures; song lyrics for cultural context

---

### Capoeira History (capoeirahistory.com)

**URL:** https://capoeirahistory.com
**Languages:** English (primary)
**Focus:** Historical mestres with emphasis on Rio de Janeiro lineages, research-backed biographies
**Reliability:** High - emphasizes research-backed content with project consultants

**Data Available:**
- Birth/death years with precision
- Portrait photographs
- Generational classifications (1st, 2nd generation)
- Teacher-student relationships
- Biographical narratives
- Rio de Janeiro historical context

**How to Access:**
- Masters A-Z index: `capoeirahistory.com/masters-a-z-3/`
- Individual pages: `capoeirahistory.com/mestre/[master-name-and-years]/`
  - Example: `capoeirahistory.com/mestre/master-dentinho-1952-2011/`
- Filter by generation or surname initial

**Best For:** Rio de Janeiro mestres, generational lineages, 20th century figures, cross-referencing Bahian sources

**Note:** Complements velhosmestres.com (Bahia-focused) with Rio de Janeiro perspective

---

### Papoeira (papoeira.com)

**URL:** https://papoeira.com
**Languages:** Portuguese (primary), English (/en/)
**Focus:** In-depth biographical articles, historical research
**Reliability:** Medium-High - well-researched articles

**Data Available:**
- Detailed biographies with narrative
- Training companions and associates
- Death circumstances (for historical figures)
- Cultural context

**How to Access:**
- Person: `papoeira.com/en/who-was-[name]/` or search

**Best For:** Besouro Mangangá, historical figures, death narratives, cultural context

## Tier 2: Community Directories

### Portal Capoeira (portalcapoeira.com)

**URL:** https://portalcapoeira.com/diretorio-de-academias/
**Focus:** Academia directory with lineage field
**Reliability:** Medium - user-contributed

**Data Available:**
- 548+ academias listed
- "Linhagem" field (lineage - free text)
- "Responsável" field (leader name)
- Location with coordinates
- Contact information (phone, website)
- Tags and categories

**How to Access:**
- Directory: `portalcapoeira.com/diretorio-de-academias/`
- Search by location, tag, or keyword

**Best For:** Finding groups by location, identifying group leadership, lineage keywords

**Note:** Lineage is free-text, must parse manually

---

### IPHAN Brazil Registry (capoeira.iphan.gov.br)

**URL:** https://capoeira.iphan.gov.br/grupo
**Focus:** Official Brazilian government cultural heritage registry
**Reliability:** High - official government data

**Data Available:**
- 1,446+ registered groups
- Entity type (confederation, federation, league, association, school, group)
- Municipality, state, region
- Style classification
- Cultural designation (ponto de cultura, ponto de memória)
- Has headquarters (boolean)

**How to Access:**
- Browse by state, municipality, entity type, style
- Note: Primarily Brazil-focused

**Best For:** Brazilian groups, official entity type classification, cultural heritage status

**Limitations:** No lineage data, no mestre profiles, Brazil-centric

---

### CapoeiraWiki (capoeirawiki.org)

**URL:** https://capoeirawiki.org
**Focus:** Encyclopedia-style documentation
**Reliability:** Medium - community wiki

**Data Available:**
- Mestre biographies (narrative)
- Organization descriptions
- Definitions (what is a "mestre"?)
- Historical context

**How to Access:**
- Articles: `capoeirawiki.org/wiki/[Name]`
- Categories: `capoeirawiki.org/wiki/Category:[Topic]`

**Best For:** Definitions, context, narrative descriptions

**Limitations:** No structured relationships, can't query "all students of X"

---

### Capoeira Fandom Wiki (capoeira.fandom.com)

**URL:** https://capoeira.fandom.com
**Focus:** Wiki documentation, group logos
**Reliability:** Low-Medium - community wiki

**Data Available:**
- Group pages with logos
- Category-based organization (e.g., "Filhos de Bimba")
- Basic descriptions

**How to Access:**
- Groups: `capoeira.fandom.com/wiki/[Group_Name]`
- Categories: `capoeira.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Groupes`

**Best For:** Group logos, category exploration

**Limitations:** Only 29 groups, limited data

---

**Data Available:**
- Mestre biographies

## Tier 4: Academic & Historical Sources

### IPHAN Cultural Registry

**URL:** https://capoeira.iphan.gov.br
**Focus:** Cultural heritage documentation
**Reliability:** High - government

**Data Available:**
- Official cultural heritage status
- Research publications
- Historical documentation

**Best For:** Cultural significance context, official recognition

---

### Academic Papers (via Web Search)

**Search Terms:**
- "[mestre name] capoeira academic"
- "capoeira history [topic]"
- "capoeira angola [name]"
- "[name] IPHAN"

**Sources to Look For:**
- University research (UFBA, USP, etc.)
- Dissertations and theses
- Journal articles (Afro-Asia, CPDOC, etc.)

**Best For:** Historical figures, academic citations, primary source references

---

### Books (Reference Only)

Notable academic works frequently cited:

- **Waldeloir Rego** - "Capoeira Angola: Ensaio Sócio-Etnográfico" (1968)
- **Frede Abreu** - Various works on Bahian capoeira history
- **Nestor Capoeira** - "The Little Capoeira Book", "A Street-Smart Song"
- **Pedro Abib** - "Capoeira Angola: Cultura Popular e o Jogo dos Saberes na Roda" (2009)
- **Carlos Eugênio Líbano Soares** - "A Negregada Instituição"

**Note:** Cannot WebFetch book content; use for context and to identify what to search for

## Sources NOT to Use

These sources are problematic for genealogy data:

- **Social media profiles** - Ephemeral, not authoritative
- **Random blog posts** - No verification
- **SEO content farms** - Often plagiarized, inaccurate
- **Group affiliate sites claiming lineage** - Verification needed

Always cross-reference claims with Tier 1 sources when possible.
