# Mestra Sandrinha / Sandra Eugênia Feitosa (b. 1959)

**Significance:** First woman to achieve the rank of mestra in capoeira in Brazil.

**Profile Type:** `pioneering_mestra`

| Affiliation | Grupo Bantus de Capoeira (founded by Mestre Roque); Grupo Sabata |

**Biography:**
Sandrinha was born in the favela of Pavãozinho in Rio de Janeiro in 1959. She trained at Grupo Bantus de Capoeira, founded by Mestre Roque, and participated in shows with Grupo Sabata. She was recognized as mestra in the 1970s - the first woman in Brazil to achieve this rank.

**Teacher:**
- Mestre Roque (Grupo Bantus)
