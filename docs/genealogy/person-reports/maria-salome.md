# Maria Salomé (1920s-1930s)

**Significance:** Friend and possibly student of Maria Doze Homens; celebrated in Mestre Pastinha's song.

**Profile Type:** `legendary_capoeirista`

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Apelido | Maria Salomé |
| Era | 1920s-1930s |
| Location | Bahia |

**Biography:**
Maria Salomé was a friend of Maria Doze Homens. According to Mestre Atenilo, Maria Doze Homens was one of the most famous women in capoeira of the past and **Maria Salomé was her student**.

The song "Adão, Adão, oi cadê Salomé, Adão" on Mestre Pastinha's LP is said to refer to Maria Salomé.

**Teacher:**
- Maria Doze Homens (according to Mestre Atenilo)
