# Doze Homens (late 19th - early 20th century)

**Significance:** Among Besouro's contemporaries and friends who participated in simulated fights with the sound of the berimbau and pandeiro.

**Profile Type:** `proto_mestre`

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Apelido | Doze Homens ("Twelve Men") |
| Location | Santo Amaro da Purificação, Bahia |
| Role | Capoeira teacher, friend of Besouro |

**Biography:**
Doze Homens was among Besouro's contemporaries and friends who participated in simulated fights with the sound of the berimbau and pandeiro.

**Note:** There is also a legendary woman, Maria Doze Homens, who may have been connected to this mestre (see Women section).
