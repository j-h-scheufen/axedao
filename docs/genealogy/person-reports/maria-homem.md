# Maria Homem (early 20th century)

**Significance:** Infamous street-fighting capoeirista from Bahia.

**Profile Type:** `legendary_capoeirista`

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Apelido | Maria Homem ("Maria the Man") |
| Location | Pelourinho, Salvador, Bahia |

**Biography:**
According to Mestre Pastinha, Maria Homem liked to drink at a bar on Pelourinho Square. One time, three police officers tried to arrest her for being drunk and disorderly, and she managed to knock them all to the ground one after the other.
