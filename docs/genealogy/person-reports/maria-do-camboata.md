# Maria do Camboatá (early 20th century)

**Significance:** Legendary female capoeirista from Bahia.

**Profile Type:** `legendary_capoeirista`

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Apelido | Maria do Camboatá (also spelled Comboatá) |
| Location | Bahia |

**Biography:**
Dona Maria do Camboatá is a legendary female capoeirista purported to have been a formidable fighter in early 20th century Bahia. She is not to be confused with Maria Doze Homens, Maria Homem, or Maria Felipa de Oliveira.
