# Nicolau do Poço (late 19th - early 20th century)

**Significance:** Famous capoeira malta leader of Recife carnival.

**Profile Type:** `historical_capoeirista`

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Apelido | Nicolau do Poço |
| Location | Recife, Pernambuco |
| Role | Malta leader |

**Biography:**
Nicolau do Poço was among the famous capoeiras whose names are preserved in the history of Recife's carnival. He was one of the "moleques de banda de música" (band boys) and malta leaders gradually eliminated by police until the suppression of the maltas around 1912.
