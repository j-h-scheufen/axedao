# Maria Doze Homens (early 20th century)

**Significance:** Legendary female capoeirista of Bahia; renowned for defeating multiple police officers.

**Profile Type:** `legendary_capoeirista`

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Apelido | Maria Doze Homens ("Maria Twelve Men") |
| Era | 1920s-1930s |
| Location | Bahia |

**Biography:**
Maria Doze Homens and her friend Maria Salomé are said to have been two friends who loved samba and capoeira and were renowned for causing trouble in Bahia in the 1920s and 1930s.

The origin of her nickname is disputed:
- Some say she defeated (or killed) 12 police officers in a fight
- Others say she trained with a Mestre Doze Homens
- Others say she was a friend and sidekick of Besouro Mangangá
