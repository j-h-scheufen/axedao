## Oral Transmission Networks

### NETWORK 1: African-Born Teachers → First Brazilian Generation

The deepest documented layer consists of African-born teachers who brought fighting traditions to Brazil:

| Teacher | Origin | Student | When | Notes |
|---------|--------|---------|------|-------|
| Benedito | Angola | Pastinha | ~1899 | "negro velho africano" |
| Bentinho | Africa | Bimba | ~1911 | Ship captain |
| Tio Alípio | Africa | Besouro | ~1900s | Worked at sugar mill |
| Cândido Pequeno | Angola | Noronha | 1917 | "negro Angola" |

### NETWORK 2: The Santo Amaro Hub (Recôncavo)

Santo Amaro da Purificação was the crucible of legendary capoeira:

- **Besouro Mangangá** (1897-1924) - the legendary figure
- **Cobrinha Verde** (1908-1983) - Besouro's cousin and student
- Multiple mestres trained together informally

### NETWORK 3: The Gengibirra Academy

Founded ~1920s in Salvador, Gengibirra was the first organized center of Capoeira Angola:

**The Four Owners:**
- Mestre Amorzinho
- Mestre Noronha
- Mestre Livino
- Mestre Totonho de Maré

**Other Founding Mestres (~21 total):**
Aberrê, Geraldo Chapeleiro, Juvenal Engraxate, Onça Preta, Bigode de Seda, Percílio, Geraldo Pé de Abelha, Zehí, Bom Nome, Henrique Cara Queimada, Cimento, Argemiro Grande, Galindeu, Boca de Porco, Lúcio Pequeno, Paquete do Cabula

### NETWORK 4: Salvador Hub - Interconnected Academies (1940s-1960s)

In Salvador, multiple academies formed an interconnected network:
- CECA (Pastinha) - founded 1941
- Academia de Bimba - founded 1932
- Various informal training spaces

Students often trained at multiple locations, creating cross-lineage connections.

## Why the Documentation Gap?

Capoeira's history has significant gaps because:

1. **Oral tradition:** Capoeira was passed through practice, not writing
2. **Criminalization:** Practitioners hid their identities (1890-1937)
3. **Slavery records:** Enslaved people were often unnamed in records
4. **Police bias:** Records focus on arrests, not lineages
5. **Academic neglect:** Serious research began only in the 1960s-70s

This makes every documented name precious - and requires careful handling of uncertainty.
