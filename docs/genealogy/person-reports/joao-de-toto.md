# João de Totó (late 19th - early 20th century)

**Significance:** Famous capoeira figure of Recife; arrested during suppression campaigns.

**Profile Type:** `historical_capoeirista`

| Field | Value |

**Biography:**
João de Totó was one of the famous capoeiras mentioned alongside Jovino dos Coelhos and Nicolau do Poço. He was sent to detention during police campaigns against the capoeiras of Recife. The police campaign sent João de Totó, Valdevino, and Jovino dos Coelhos to detention, others to Fernando de Noronha (prison island), and others directly to the morgue.
