# Diogo da Lapa / Diogo Francisco de Oliveira (late 19th century)

**Significance:** Capoeirista arrested for participation in the Guarda Negra.

**Biography:**
Fernão Diogo, alias Diogo da Lapa, participated in the Guarda Negra of Largo da Lapa, along with ten capoeiras in the Santana neighborhood. He was arrested in mid-December 1889 following the Proclamation of the Republic.
