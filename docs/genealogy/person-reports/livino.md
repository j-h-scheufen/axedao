# Mestre Livino / Livinho (early 20th century)

**Significance:** Brother of Mestre Noronha; one of the four "owners and proprietors" of Gengibirra; co-managed Centro de Capoeira Angola da Conceição da Praia.

**Profile Type:** `early_mestre`

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Apelido | Livino, Livinho |
| Relation | Brother of Mestre Noronha (Daniel Coutinho) |
| Location | Liberdade, Salvador, Bahia |
| Role | Founder of Gengibirra |

**Biography:**
Mestre Livino was the brother of Mestre Noronha. Together with Noronha, Amorzinho, and Totonho de Maré, he was one of the four "donos e proprietários" (owners and proprietors) of the Centro de Capoeira Angola at Gengibirra.

He and Mestre Noronha also managed the "Centro de Capoeira Angola da Conceição da Praia."
