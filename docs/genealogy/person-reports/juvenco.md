# Mestre Juvenço (late 19th - early 20th century)

**Significance:** Blacksmith and friend of Besouro who shared stories about him; met by Mestre João Pequeno at Fazenda de São Pedro.

**Profile Type:** `proto_mestre`

**Biography:**
According to Mestre João Pequeno: "In the fazenda de São Pedro, I met Mestre Juvenço, who worked as a blacksmith (ferreiro); he was a friend of Besouro, he told me a lot of stories about Besouro".
