# Sete Coroas (late 19th - early 20th century)

**Significance:** Famous malandro who taught Madame Satã; link between generations.

**Profile Type:** `historical_capoeirista`

| Field | Value |

**Biography:**
Sete Coroas was a famous malandro (hustler/street figure) who was one of Madame Satã's teachers. We know almost nothing about him or his companions.
