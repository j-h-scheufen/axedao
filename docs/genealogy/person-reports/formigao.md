# Formigão (early 20th century)

**Significance:** Valente of Recife; involved in the 1907 train disturbance.

**Profile Type:** `historical_capoeirista`

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Apelido | Formigão |
| Location | Recife, Pernambuco |
| Era | Active early 1900s |

**Biography:**
Formigão was one of those responsible, along with Adama and Eleutério, for disturbances on a train returning from a religious festival in 1907.
