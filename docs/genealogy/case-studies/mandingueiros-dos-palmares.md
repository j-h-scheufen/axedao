## The Name - "Mandingueiros dos Palmares"

### "Mandingueiros"

**Mandinga**:
- Candomblé ritual for protection and opening paths
- Associated with mystical/spiritual power
- "Mandingueiro" = one who has/uses mandinga
- Common term in capoeira for skilled, tricky player

**"Dos Palmares"**

**Quilombo dos Palmares**:
- Most famous quilombo (maroon settlement) in Brazilian history
- 17th century settlement of escaped enslaved Africans
- Located in Alagoas, northeastern Brazil
- Leader: Zumbi dos Palmares (symbol of Afro-Brazilian resistance)
- Lasted nearly 100 years before Portuguese destruction

**Combined Meaning**: "Those with Mandinga/Magic from Palmares"
- References both spiritual power and historical resistance
- Connects to Afro-Brazilian heritage and freedom struggle
- Symbol of cultural pride and strength
